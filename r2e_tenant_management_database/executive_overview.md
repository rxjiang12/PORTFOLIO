R2E, LLC is a building management company that has owned and operated several residential properties in Long Island City, Queens for the past five years. Currently, the company relies on large Excel spreadsheets to track properties, tenants, service requests, and employees. As the business has grown, these spreadsheets have become increasingly difficult to manage, leading to inefficiencies, limited visibility into operations, and higher risk of data errors. To address this, the goal of this project was to design and implement a centralized relational database using Microsoft Access to replace the existing manual tracking system.

R2E owns multiple buildings, each containing several apartment units. Every unit can be rented to one primary tenant, although multiple occupants may reside in the same apartment under a single lease. The system must track unit occupancy status (occupied or vacant), tenant information such as name, contact details, and household size, and maintain clear relationships between properties, units, and tenants.

In addition to tenant management, R2E provides in-house repair and maintenance services, including appliance repairs, plumbing issues, and leak resolution. The database must record which apartment submitted a service request, the type of issue reported, and the employee assigned to resolve it. While employees may handle multiple service requests over time, each individual request is assigned to a single employee to ensure accountability and tracking.

The system also maintains detailed employee records, including contact information, home address, and job role, enabling accurate service assignment and operational oversight. Overall, the database is designed to improve data organization, increase operational efficiency, and provide structured reporting to support better management decisions.
